About

I'm Mark Bridges. I spent three years as CEO at Athletica.ai, where I led AI product development, managed organizational change, and built data-driven workflows that scaled growth. I also held strategic advisory roles at Basikon and Spideo, spanning product strategy, business development, and fundraising.

Now, through Bridges Strategy, I focus on helping nonprofits and mission-driven organizations figure out where AI actually moves the needle, and where it doesn't. My approach emphasizes clarity, proportionality, and trust over speed or technical novelty. This is not a software sales process or a push toward automation at all costs — in many cases, the right outcome is deciding to move slowly, limit use to internal workflows, or postpone adoption entirely until conditions are right.

"Mark has a rare ability to translate complex technical and strategic topics into clear, actionable insights. He balanced innovation with long-term organizational value, ensuring technology served strategy rather than the other way around." Paul Laursen, Co-Founder, Athletica
