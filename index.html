Bridges Strategy

Practical AI strategy for nonprofits and charities with limited resources.

What I do

I help nonprofit organizations think clearly and responsibly about how artificial intelligence can support their mission without overwhelming their teams or compromising trust.

This work often starts with governance and readiness, then moves toward low-lift, high-impact uses of existing tools, and only later into more advanced workflows when appropriate.

How I help

  • Board and leadership discussions on AI readiness and risk
  • Development of practical, values-aligned AI policies
  • Identification of low-effort opportunities to reduce staff burden
  • Guided experimentation with tools and workflows

About

I work at the intersection of product strategy, artificial intelligence, and organizational decision-making. My background includes building and advising technology products, and supporting mission-driven organizations that operate with limited capacity and high trust obligations.

I focus on clarity, restraint, and practical judgment rather than technology for its own sake.
